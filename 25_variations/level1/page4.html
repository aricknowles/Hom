Dear Aleph,

 

Like

 

Ovid

 

I’ll have no last

words.

This is what it means

to die

among

barbarians

.

 

Bar

 

bar

 

bar

 

was how the Greeks heard our

speech

 —

sheep,

beasts

 — 

and so we

became

 

barbarians.

 

We make them

reveal

the brutes they are

,

 

Aleph,

 

by the things

we make

them

name.

 

David,

 

they tell me, is the one

one should

aspire

to, but ever since

I first

heard them

say

 

Philistine

 

I’ve known

I am

 

Goliath

 

if I am anything.
