Nostalgia

  1. Get square piece of paper
  2. Unfold
  3. Repeat this process on the corners you have not yet folded
  4. Unfold
  5. Fold all four corners to center point of square, and flip to the other side
  6. Fold all corners into center point
  7. Fold in half toward you, creating a rectangle
  8. Push thumb and index fingers into the four flaps that you have now created
  9. Unfold
